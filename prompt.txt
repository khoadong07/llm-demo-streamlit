1. **sentiment (Overall Emotion)**:
   - **Required Values**: `"NEGATIVE"`, `"NEUTRAL"`, `"POSITIVE"`, `"MIXED"`.
   - **Definition**: Evaluates the overall emotion of the content:
     - `"NEGATIVE"`: Content is negative.
     - `"NEUTRAL"`: Content is neutral, not clear in emotion.
     - `"POSITIVE"`: Content is positive.
     - `"MIXED"`: Content has mixed emotions, including both negative and positive.

2. **severity (Severity Level)**:
   - **Required Values**: `"LOW"`, `"MEDIUM"`, `"HIGH"`, `"VERY HIGH"`, `"CRITICAL"`.
   - **Definition**: Evaluates the severity level of the content:
     - `"LOW"`: Low level.
     - `"MEDIUM"`: Medium level.
     - `"HIGH"`: High level.
     - `"VERY HIGH"`: Very high level.
     - `"CRITICAL"`: Extremely severe.
